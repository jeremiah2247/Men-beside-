About Us

Men Beside BPD is a peer-led support group for men whose partners live with BPD. We offer a safe, confidential space to share experiences, develop healthy boundaries, and support one another with empathy and respect.

Our Mission

"To provide non-judgmental support, foster emotional resilience, and build community among men in relationships touched by BPD—without blame, stigma, or shame."

What We Offer

  • Weekly Online Meetings on Zoom (private & facilitated) from 7–8 PM every Tuesday
